Real-Time Heading-Aware Terrain Mobility Estimation for Off-Road AVs
WPI AVMI · paper in prep, targeting IEEE RA-L
project page →

Real-time off-road terrain mobility estimation: a physics-supervised CNN that turns a slow physics-simulation baseline (~30 min per multi-heading map) into millisecond-per-patch inference at 0.89 F1 on unseen terrain. Trained entirely on synthetic data from a custom fractal terrain generator rendered in Unreal Engine and physics-labeled, then deployed live as a ROS2 perception node publishing per-heading traversability inside a full off-road autonomy stack.

0.89 F1 10–20 ms / patch real-time ROS2 node synthetic-trained
Sensor Simulation & ROS2 Integration in Unreal Engine
WPI AVMI
project page →

A custom sensor-simulation stack in Unreal Engine that generates unlimited labeled training data at zero cost: a raycast-based 3D spinning LiDAR and a camera with in-engine semantic segmentation, both streaming over ROS2. Required integrating rclUE into Unreal Engine 5.4 and fixing plugin build incompatibilities to get ROS2 publishing working on the updated engine.

3D LiDAR model semantic camera ROS2 / rclUE Unreal Engine 5.4
Lensless Bio-Inspired Stereo Vision for Micro Aerial Vehicles
WPI PEAR Lab
project page →

A from-scratch lensless stereo camera — pinholes and a microlens array over a bare CMOS, no lenses — for ultra-light MAVs, where two overlapping views land as one blended image to be split into stereo. I trained a dual-decoder U-Net on data I generated without ground truth via an on-screen homography calibration, then built a servo-multiplexed rig to capture real stereo with epipolar geometric supervision. I made the call to stop the project when the achievable disparity showed it couldn't beat a conventional stereo camera on cost — the premise that justified the whole approach.

~50M-param U-Net ~0.84 val SSIM servo-multiplexed rig ~12px disp @ 0.5m

Deep and Un-Deep Visual-Inertial Odometry
WPI · 2025

A head-to-head of classical vs. learned VIO. A stereo MSCKF on the error-state manifold hits 0.094 m translational RMSE on EuRoC MH-01; a learned dual-stream network (CNN + LSTM) regressing 6-DoF pose did not recover metric scale — a concrete demonstration of why scale is unobservable under loose visual-inertial fusion.
