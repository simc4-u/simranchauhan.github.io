Real-Time Heading-Aware Terrain Mobility Estimation for Off-Road AVs
WPI Autonomous Vehicle Mobility Institute · paper in prep, targeting IEEE RA-L
project page →

Real-time off-road terrain mobility estimation: a physics-supervised CNN that turns a slow physics-simulation baseline (~30 min per multi-heading map) into millisecond-per-patch inference at 0.89 F1 on unseen terrain. Trained entirely on synthetic data from a custom fractal terrain generator rendered in Unreal Engine and physics-labeled, then deployed live as a ROS2 perception node publishing per-heading traversability inside a full off-road autonomy stack.

Lensless Bio-Inspired Stereo Vision for Micro Aerial Vehicles
WPI Perception and Autonomous Robotics (PEAR) Lab
project page →

Designed a single-pinhole lensless MLA camera that captures multiplexed overlapping views, formulating stereo reconstruction as a supervised demultiplexing problem. I generated 20K synthetic training pairs via a distance-invariant homography, then built a two-pinhole servo-actuated rig on an NVIDIA Jetson that selectively exposes two MLA lenslets to acquire aligned blended/left/right triplets for real-data supervision. Trained a ~50M-parameter dual-decoder U-Net (L1, SSIM, reconstruction, and epipolar + homography regularization) to demultiplex blended captures into stereo for depth, then made a principled call to stop when the sub-3mm baseline's achievable disparity couldn't beat conventional stereo on cost.

~50M-param U-Net ~0.84 val SSIM servo-multiplexed rig ~12px disp @ 0.5m
Controllable Depth-of-Field in Gaussian Splatting (VizFlyt UAV Simulator)
WPI Perception and Autonomous Robotics (PEAR) Lab
project page →

Implemented controllable depth-of-field in a 3D Gaussian Splatting model (Splatfacto) with learnable focal-distance and aperture parameters jointly optimized alongside the scene geometry. Engineered a differentiable CUDA bokeh kernel (custom autograd.Function with hand-derived gradients) modeling physically accurate thin-lens defocus via a depth-based circle-of-confusion, composited in linear gamma-corrected space — extending the lab's open-source VizFlyt UAV simulator.

Deep and Un-Deep Visual-Inertial Odometry
WPI · 2025

A head-to-head of classical vs. learned VIO. A stereo MSCKF on the error-state manifold hits 0.094 m translational RMSE on EuRoC MH-01; a learned dual-stream network (CNN + LSTM) regressing 6-DoF pose did not recover metric scale — a concrete demonstration of why scale is unobservable under loose visual-inertial fusion.
